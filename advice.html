How to recycle in Cyprus

Recyclation in Cyprus is not as hard as you may think. It is more the absence of any campaign about recyclation and unawarenes of the public, that stops it from becoming more popular.

How to recycle (except glass)

  1. Take a special recycling bag for paper and PMD (plastic, metal, drink containers) from your local supermarket
  2. Put designated waste into seperate bags
  3. Put the bags out whenever they are full but preferably during the pickup day
    4. You can find a pickup day for your address on this map of cyprus green dot

How to recycle glass

  1. Find the nearest glass container to your home at this map of cyprus green dot
  2. Take your glass bottles and jars to this container
  3. That is it, super easy!
