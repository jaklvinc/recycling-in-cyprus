Cyprus is one of the worst countries in the EU ragarding total production of waste per person for a year. Not only that, but also in the amount of thrash that goes to landfills in contrast with how much is reused and recycled.
Today in times, when global warming is getting worse every year, and when there are floating islands made of plastics the size of Mexico in the Pacific ocean, it is more important than ever, to start thinking about your responsibility in this mattter. And it is especially you, the younger generation, that will feel the results of your actions the most in the future.
